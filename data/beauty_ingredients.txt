niacinamide: reduces oil production and improves pores. Good for oily and acne-prone skin.

azelaic acid: helps treat acne, redness, and rosacea.

retinol: increases cell turnover and reduces wrinkles.

salicylic acid: exfoliates pores and treats acne.

hyaluronic acid: hydrates skin and improves moisture barrier.

vitamin c: brightens skin and reduces hyperpigmentation.

benzoyl peroxide: kills acne-causing bacteria.
